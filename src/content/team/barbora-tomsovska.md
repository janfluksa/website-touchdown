Barbora was one of the first headhunters in Prague and brings over 20 years of experience in Executive Search. Since the 1990s, she has worked with an international headhunting firm and later, as Partner, led its operations across Central and Eastern Europe. Barbora founded Touchdown with the goal of delivering top-tier Executive Search services and leadership development programmes for senior executives. She specialises in recruiting senior leaders in banking, private equity, telecommunications, FMCG, and pharmaceuticals. She holds an MBA from Sheffield Hallam University and is fluent in Czech, English, Spanish, and French.
